Particle and Astroparticle Physics Autumn Programme

We are organizing the event


Who

40 physics students, from the Italian Association of Physics Students and the International Association of Physics Students. All background and nationalities are welcome!

When

Between the 4th and the 8th of October 2016.

Where

In central Italy. Students will meet in Rome and visit the INFN laboratories in Frascati on the first day. They will then move to the city of L'Aquila to spend two days at the LNGS and GSSI facilities.

  • LNGS

    Gran Sasso National Laboratory

  • INFN

    Italian National Agency for Nuclear Physics

  • GSSI

    Gran Sasso Science Institute

The Particle & Astroparticle Physics Programme, now at its second edition, is organised by the Italian Association of Physics Students and is open to all members of the International Assocation of Physics Students. The event will bring 40 students to visit some of the most advanced research facilities in Europe, with a focus on astroparticle physics and one key mission: to foster enthusiasm in the minds of young physicists.

Associazione Italiana Studenti di Fisica

The Associazione Italiana Studenti di Fisica (AISF) gathers together all the Italian students of physics. It was founded in Heidelberg in August 2014 and since January 2015 is a National Committee for the International Associazion of Physics Students (IAPS).
